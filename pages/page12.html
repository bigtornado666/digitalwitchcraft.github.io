The Justice

The image shows a rusty mass balance scale weighing two computers. One computer's screen is showing a feather, the other one is showing a heart.

When this card appears, it signifies the correctness and rationality of the situation. It also represents approaching matters with a sense of justice, considering a moderate balance, pursuing harmony, fairness, equity, maintaining impartiality, and striving for a state of equilibrium both in handling situations and maintaining a mindset. Additionally, this card signifies virtues such as personal reputation and chastity, all of which receive unequivocally positive affirmation. The actions are, at the very least, in accordance with established norms, demonstrating self-restraint.

In the digital era, the embodiment of fairness and justice is evident in various aspects. Firstly, blockchain technology and smart contracts provide the potential for decentralized transactions, enabling fair decision-making without the need for intermediaries. Secondly, social media's public scrutiny has become a driving force for promoting fairness and justice. Users contribute to creating a more inclusive and fair social environment by exposing false information and discriminatory speech. Additionally, open-source software and the principles of knowledge sharing encourage openness in the tech industry, preventing monopolies and fostering fair competition. Digital identity and privacy protection technologies focus on safeguarding individual rights, assisting individuals in better controlling their digital identity, and reducing data misuse and discrimination. These innovations suggest that the convergence of technology and society in the electronic arts has the potential to shape a more just, transparent, and inclusive digital society.
